About Me

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Library and Information Science at The University of Zambia (UNZA), where I teach Information Sciences. I conduct research in Applied Computer Science, with a bias towards Education---Presently, my main research interests lie in computer science education, digital libraries, learning analytics, and technology-enhanced learning.

Before joining UNZA, I was a Ph.D student (thesis under examination) at the University of Cape Town (UCT).
Prior to that, I was an M.Sc. student at UCT.
Formerly, I worked with Telco ETL processing nodes at Airtel.
